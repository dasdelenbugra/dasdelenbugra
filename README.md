### About me

I'm a Computer Engineering graduate from Tokat Gaziosmanpaşa University with a focus on **AI/ML** and **Android development**. I enjoy building end-to-end solutions — from training models to deploying them inside mobile apps.

My graduation project, **Market Rehberim**, combines Computer Vision with Android to let users photograph a product and compare prices across local markets. That project sits at the intersection of everything I love: real computer vision, mobile UX, and a tangible real-world problem.

- 📍 Aydın, Turkey — open to remote opportunities
- 🎓 B.Sc. Computer Engineering — GPA 82.47/100 (final semester: 94.54/100)
- 💬 Ask me about: Computer Vision, RAG pipelines, Android + AI integration
